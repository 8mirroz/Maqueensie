# Offers library

## Pattern 1 — Fixed estimate + deadline + guarantee
- Seen at: Remont.fm, Domeo, RemontProfi
- Problem solved: fear of budget overrun and delays
- Why it works: moves offer from craft uncertainty to operational certainty
- Adaptation potential: very high

## Pattern 2 — Design project as gift / refund
- Seen at: ArtRemont, Fundament, sometimes bundled in others
- Problem solved: makes premium planning step feel free
- Why it works: reduces friction on entering full-cycle service
- Adaptation potential: high

## Pattern 3 — Phased / post-fact payment
- Seen at: Domeo, Remont.fm, RemontProfi
- Problem solved: trust deficit and fear of losing money upfront
- Why it works: turns contractor into lower-risk choice
- Adaptation potential: very high

## Pattern 4 — Procurement / materials / furniture / acceptance as extensions
- Seen at: Domeo, Vira-ArtStroy, SK Nebo
- Problem solved: client overload and coordination fatigue
- Why it works: upgrades average contract value and locks the client deeper into one ecosystem
- Adaptation potential: very high

## Pattern 5 — Digital control / weekly media reports
- Seen at: Domeo, SK Nebo
- Problem solved: lack of visibility for absent owners / busy clients
- Why it works: translates invisible process into reassuring proof
- Adaptation potential: high

## Pattern 6 — Premium bundle: engineering + smart home + custom furniture + insurance
- Seen at: Vira-ArtStroy
- Problem solved: premium clients want one accountable integrator
- Why it works: creates a true premium moat beyond basic renovation labor
- Adaptation potential: medium/high

## Pattern 7 — Narrow landing pages by apartment type / size / developer
- Seen at: Domeo, RemontProfi, Remont.fm
- Problem solved: better relevance for search and conversion
- Why it works: high demand capture strength in SEO and ad traffic
- Adaptation potential: very high
