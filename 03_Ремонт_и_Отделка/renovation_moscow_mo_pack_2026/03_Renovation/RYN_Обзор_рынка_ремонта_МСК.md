# Market structure

## Segment map
### 1. Mass / performance lead generation
Typical promise:
- price from
- free estimate
- fast start
- fixed estimate
- stage payment
- warranty

Representative players:
- Remont.fm
- RemontProfi
- SK Nebo
- ReRooms Remont

### 2. Full-cycle middle / upper-middle
Typical promise:
- design + renovation + procurement
- one contractor for the whole cycle
- client reporting
- optional furniture / acceptance / supervision

Representative players:
- Domeo
- ArtRemont
- SK Nebo

### 3. Premium design-build
Typical promise:
- project-first execution
- engineering package
- author supervision
- custom furniture / smart home / insurance / procurement

Representative players:
- Vira-ArtStroy
- Fundament
- high-end boutique studios

## Repeating demand-capture mechanics
- free measurement / estimate
- calculator on landing page
- fixed estimate or "final estimate"
- phased payment or payment by fact
- design project as a gift / refund
- procurement and materials support
- remote photo/video reporting
- warranty 2–5 years

## Working strategic interpretation
The market sells risk reduction more often than craftsmanship itself. The most repeated winning structure is not "cheap repair" but:
1. predictable budget
2. single accountable contractor
3. visible control of deadlines and procurement
4. warranty and legal certainty

## Opportunity gaps
- separate product ladder for:
  - works only
  - works + rough materials
  - full turnkey
- clearer split by:
  - new build / white box
  - secondary housing
  - houses / townhouses
  - Moscow city / Moscow Oblast logistics
- stronger client dashboard with:
  - change-order log
  - procurement status
  - budget snapshot
  - weekly media reports
  - milestone acceptance
