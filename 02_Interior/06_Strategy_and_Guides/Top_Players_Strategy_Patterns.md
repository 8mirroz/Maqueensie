# 12 repeating patterns

| pattern | what top players do | Russia signal | Global signal | adaptation value |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1. Office as business tool | продают пространство как productivity, attraction, retention tool | VK Tech | Gensler survey / M Moser / BCG Dubai | very high |
| 2. Signature common zones | делают входные, лаунж, café, amphitheatre, forum spaces | VK Tech, Smart Group | WeBank, On, Space House | very high |
| 3. Local identity | связывают интерьер с местом и контекстом | Aeroflot flagship / Russian brand layer | BCG Dubai, Amazon Cape Town | very high |
| 4. Space for collaboration | усиливают сценарии общения и со-творчества | Kept, VK Tech | Gensler research, WeBank, Joye | very high |
| 5. Acoustic / tech maturity | закладывают кабины, панели, booking, VCS, workplace apps | Kept, Aeroflot | high-performing workplace logic globally | very high |
| 6. Hospitality logic | офис и шоурум проектируются как experience space | MultispacePravda, Sreda | retail gallery / convene / flexible offices | very high |
| 7. Layered sustainability | sustainability не как значок, а как материал, воздух, вода, retrofit | emerging | WeBank, Amazon Cape Town, Space House | high |
| 8. Full-stack execution | показывают architecture + engineering + construction + suppliers | Kept, Natsproektstroy | common in global award entries | very high |
| 9. Brand storytelling | используют проект как narrative about company | Aeroflot, Smart Group | SIGMA Space, BCG Dubai | very high |
| 10. Adaptive reuse / retrofit | превращают существующий объект в новый актив | точечно | Neri&Hu, Space House | high |
| 11. Employee inclusion | вовлекают пользователей и строят people-first offices | emerging | Gensler 2025 survey | very high |
| 12. Showroom as conversion engine | шоурум = curated sales environment | Sreda, Smart Group | SIGMA Space | very high |

# Strategic implications for Moscow/MO platform

## What to standardize in your product system
- Office / showroom briefs should include:
  - work modes,
  - client journey,
  - brand narrative,
  - acoustic-tech stack,
  - collaboration formats,
  - hospitality zones,
  - sustainability story,
  - procurement logic.

## What to show on site / in sales materials
- before/after logic
- floorplan story
- business task → design solution map
- supplier / material / tech stack
- guarantees, deadlines, budget logic
- photos of common zones first, not just random beauty shots

## What top players rarely show well (opportunity gap)
- ROI / KPI after occupancy
- transparent package normalization
- quantified outcomes for hiring, productivity, client impression
- modular offers for SMB clients
