# Supply Map — China, Local Workshops, Replica, Original, Antique

## A. China / OEM / ODM
### Best-fit categories
- lighting
- decor
- outdoor furniture
- chairs
- side tables
- hardware-heavy modular items
- accessories
- private-label collections

### Advantages
- breadth of assortment
- flexible OEM/ODM
- fast testing of styles
- strong cost control at scale

### Risks
- customs / compliance volatility
- logistics instability
- quality drift without QC
- dependence on agents and payment routes

### Evidence
- В 2024 бизнес предупреждал о риске удорожания мебели минимум на 15% из-за роста пошлин на китайскую фурнитуру. [Confirmed Fact]
- Позже ФТС отменила повышенную пошлину, что показывает волатильность регуляторного режима. [Confirmed Fact]
- На 2026 усилился курс на обеление импорта, таможенный контроль и давление на серые схемы. [Confirmed Fact]

## B. Local small production
### Best-fit categories
- kitchens
- wardrobes
- built-ins
- veneer cabinetry
- tables
- upholstered beds
- custom sofas
- millwork

### Advantages
- customization
- faster revisions
- better fit for Moscow projects
- easier collaboration with designers
- white-label or studio-label potential

### Risks
- quality inconsistency
- weak process maturity
- variable timelines
- often weak content/marketing packaging

## C. Replica-inspired segment
### Use cases
- staged luxury look in mid-market
- hospitality or sample apartments
- budget-conscious premium aesthetics

### Strategic caution
- IP/legal risks
- fragile brand positioning
- inconsistent materials and longevity
- can undermine premium trust if not clearly framed

## D. Original designer furniture
### Use cases
- flagship interiors
- luxury apartments/houses
- showroom showcases
- PR / portfolio projects

## E. Vintage / antique / curated resale
### Use cases
- premium eclectic projects
- collector clients
- hospitality with identity
- decor layering and focal points
